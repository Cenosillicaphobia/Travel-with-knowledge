Air traffic pollution: how much does it impact on the environment?

About 2.4% of global CO2 emissions come from air traffic, according to data from the International Council on Clean Transportation (ICCT).
Everything we do, from the food we eat to the way we travel, results in the release of emissions into the atmosphere with an impact on the environment.
But there are some activities that impact more than others: around 2.4% of global CO2 emissions come from air traffic, according to data from the International Council on Clean Transportation (ICCT).
Together with other gases and water vapor produced by aircraft, the entire aviation industry is responsible for about 5% of global warming.
